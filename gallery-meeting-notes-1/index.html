Theme:

  • A focus on the young and old of Toronto with a focus on their ambition and wisdom respectively.
    • By having insight not the past and present stories of people in our city we have the ability to look towards the future
  • Touch on individual stories that have a deeper meaning

Goals:

  • The theme and feel need to stay consistent with Stiir’s overall goal to inspire and motivate others.
  • Set and reach a donation goal towards a youth cause.
  • Set a date
  • Gain interest for future gallery events (ie. Talking Raptors, Women’s topics, etc.)
    • Bring awareness to Stiir team for client projects
    • Create a platform for subjects and photographers to gain recognition
  • Create Discussion
